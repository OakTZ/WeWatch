WeWatch

Welcome!

Hello and welcome to WeWatch - the chrome extenstion that lets you watch YouTube videos with all of your friends and co-workers.

With this extenstion you can open a new watching room on any youtube video possible and you can share the rooms serial number and password to let your friends join in!
